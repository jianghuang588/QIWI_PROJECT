Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/10/25. QIWI is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their personal information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect basic information to create and manage your account:

  • Name (first name only publicly visible)
  • Email address (used for login and communication)
  • Major and selected courses
  • Optional profile descriptions

Technical Data

We automatically collect non-personal technical information:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage analytics and platform interactions
  • Connection data and IP addresses
  • Performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

Platform Features

Your data enables core platform functionality:

  • Match tutors and students based on courses
  • Enable secure internal messaging
  • Display relevant tutor recommendations
  • Maintain session history

Service Improvement

We use data to enhance your experience:

  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate platform updates
  • Analyze usage patterns

Data Protection & Your Rights

Data Security

We protect your information with industry-standard security:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data:

  • Access and update your information
  • Request data deletion
  • Modify privacy settings
  • Export your data

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing only occurs:

  • With service providers under confidentiality agreements
  • For hosting and analytics purposes
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

Policy Updates

We keep our privacy practices transparent:

  • Notify users of policy changes
  • Email alerts for significant updates
  • Platform notifications
  • Clear version history
